About Us

Welcome to Ocean Breeze Bistro! Our restaurant brings the freshest seafood and coastal flavors right to your table. Inspired by the beauty of the ocean and the rich flavors of the sea, our mission is to create memorable dining experiences for our guests.

Our Story

Founded in 2022 by Chef Marina Waters, Ocean Breeze Bistro started as a small seaside bistro with a passion for sustainable seafood and locally sourced ingredients. Today, we continue to honor that vision while creating dishes that delight both the eyes and the palate.
